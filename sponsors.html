Why sponsor us?

As a dance group, we love being able to spread our style of dance into the performing arts community. By showcasing our diverse dance form, we are able to reach large audiences and spread our story and theme yearly.

We are open to everyone - there is no dance experience required to join our family. We take full responsibility to train our dancers throughout the year. In addition, we are a support system for each other and help each other step outside of our comfort zone.

We are huge on mental and physical health. Obviously, as a dance team, we train our team physically all year. However, we use our practices to destress from school and focus on something besides academics.

Some of our members have met their best friends on Lashkara, and there is no surprise there! We are more than a team, we are a family.

We offer leadership opportunities not only as team captains, but also as commission heads of production, costume, and theme! Lashkara places a heavy emphasis on growing professionally as well with the guidance of those that are older.

As as Bollywood based dance team, we love spreading our culture on campus by hosting workshops, performing at various events, and having open practices. We hope to spread our knowledge and create a culturally competent campus!
