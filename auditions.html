Audition Info

Lashkara is back for another season, and we want you to join our family!

✶✶✶ NO DANCE EXPERIENCE NECESSARY ✶✶✶

Over the 2016-2017 season, we proved ourselves to be one of the top teams in the nation by placing 3rd at the most prestigious Bollywood Fusion competition in the world: Bollywood America. We have come so far, but this is only the beginning!

Ask anyone who has been on this team, or is currently on this team, and they will tell you that Lashkara is more than just a dance team - we are a family. By being on this family, you get to network with people all over the country, travel the world, attain a support system that will never leave your side, all while doing what we love the most: performing and telling a story through dance.

This coming October, we will be hosting our annual workshops and tryouts! At workshops, the captains will teach you a short routine that is under a minute. Whether you are trained in dance, or have never set foot into a dance studio, this is the perfect chance for you to meet new people, try something new, or even just get some exercise! At workshops, we will also have a Q & A session where we will answer any questions you have about being on the team.

If you decide to audition, we will have auditions the following few days. Captains are NOT looking for perfection - we are looking for dancers who are having fun, showing passion & energy, and take critique and implement it. We do not expect the routine to be 100% perfect. Don't let stage fright get to you! We don't bite, and we hope to make the entire audition experience fun for everyone who attends!

We're geared up for this year: now all we need is YOU! :) Check back soon for our workshops dates! We’re so excited to see you all so soon and start the Lashkara 7.0 journey!

Check back at the end of September when school starts for more information! Until then, make sure to watch this and get excited for next year :)
